Hello, I am a visual artist and front end dev who loves building things and solving problems.

I have spent most of my life making paintings and digital illustrations, and for almost half of that time I had a strong desire to learn how to code. So one day I decided to learn the art of programming.

Almost three years have passed since then and my love for creating things in the medium of code only continues to grow. I believe software will play a central role in solving the world's biggest problems and that is what I want to use it for.
